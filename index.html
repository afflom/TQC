Similarly, the Hexagon identities ensure the compatibility of the braiding isomorphism \(\gamma\) with the associator, guaranteeing that braiding one object past a tensor product of two others is equivalent to braiding it past each individually: \[\label{eq:hexagon} \alpha_{B,C,A} \circ \gamma_{A,B\otimes C} \circ \alpha_{A,B,C} = (\gamma_{A,B} \otimes id_C) \circ \alpha_{B,A,C} \circ (id_B \otimes \gamma_{A,C})\]
